Denver Critical Mass is meant to be a safe and welcoming space for all micro-modal, gas-free commuters and riders, regardless of gender, race, ability, body types, sexual orientation, or socio-economics. We value and celebrate the diversity that is Critical Mass.
It’s impossible for a ride of this size to police each and every move that individuals make on monthly Friday nights. That being said, if you see something awry, by all means, step up and say something if it doesn’t feel right, or if someone is behaving in a way that does not match the mission of Denver’s Critical Mass, which is to celebrate each other’s right to use our streets without a car, and providing safety in numbers.
This is your ride as much as it is everyone else’s, and we’re all counting on each other to keep it going.
